Seeing people struggle is difficult for me, and I don’t want another person to stay down while there’s something I can do to help. I have some experience with different people with very different influences on their health. Tiera Secrease

I have recently been struggling with my mental health and the lack of people that I feel comfortable talking about it with. I helped come up with the idea for this website while looking for things that I would have really appreciated when I was at my lowest and I hope that this provides comfort for other people like me.

Our Goal:

To help teens struggling with Mental Health, provide them with resources/ information on the importance of mental health, therapists, or generally places where you can go to get that help. Especially our website which has a forum to talk with others and relate to them. And our resources include therapists' information, where to find them, or how to get in contact with them to get help. We also have Mental Facilities/ Psychiatric Hospitals and their information so you can go get help at them.
